**6/10**

Discovery #1: Preisach hysteresis --> Ecological resilience

Magnetic materials have memory -- they resist demagnetization along a path that depends on history. We found this maps precisely onto how lakes resist returning to a clear state after eutrophication.

The Preisach density function could predict ecological recovery thresholds. No one has tried this.

**7/10**

Discovery #2: Urban innovation --> Arrhenius equation

Cities don't just follow power laws. We found that urban innovation output fits the Arrhenius form: I = rho^beta * C * exp(-Eb/S).

Population density = collision frequency. Cultural openness = temperature. Institutional barriers = activation energy.

This separates two policy levers that current scaling-law models conflate.
