# Governance

## Current model

structural-isomorphism is currently maintained by a single BDFL (Benevolent Dictator For Life) — @dada8899 — who has final say on all decisions. This model will stay in place through month 12 of the project.

## Decision-making

- **Code changes**: PR + 1 maintainer review
- **Architecture**: Architecture Decision Record (ADR) in `docs/adrs/`
- **Releases**: SemVer for `packages/*`; PRs to `release/*` branches
- **Research disputes**: opened as issues with `research` label; resolved through discussion + literature citation

## Transition to a maintainer council

A 3-member maintainer council will form when both of the following gates are met:

1. ≥10 external contributors with merged PRs over the prior quarter
2. Project age ≥12 months

At that point, council members are nominated from active contributors and confirmed by community vote. Council decisions require a 2/3 majority.
